<h1 style="color: #5e9ca0;"><u>Sword And Shield Game Instructions</u></h1>
<h2 style="color: #2e6c80;">Rules</h2>
<ul>
    <li>The yellow player starts</li>
    <li>You can create or pass on your first turn</li>
    <li>If you pass you can move or rotate any of your existing pieces at most once</li>
    <li>Any piece killed will be buried in cemetry, and you wont be able to use them anymore</li>
    <li>The objective is to get a sword to the opponents face!</li>
</ul>
<h2 style="color: #2e6c80;">Understanding Reactions</h2>
<p>A reaction can occur when a sword is touching another token and in a case of a reaction many things can happen:</p>
<p>In the case of :</p>
<ul>
    <li>Sword v Sword - both tokens die</li>
    <li>Sword v Shield - shield pushes token back</li>
    <li>Sword v Nothing - sword wins</li>
    <li>Sword v Face - player wins the game (assuming its not suicide)</li>
</ul>
<h2 style="color: #2e6c80;">Creation / Moving / Rotation</h2>
<h4 style="color: #2e6c80;">Creation</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Click on a token you want to create</li>
    <li>Click on one of the four rotations you want or click somewhere else to rechoose your token</li>
</ul>
<h4 style="color: #2e6c80;">Movement</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Click on a token you want to move</li>
    <li>A token will be highlighted with a pink square in a middle. Click on the edge outside the purple square in the direction you want to move</li>
</ul>
<h4 style="color: #2e6c80;">Rotation</h4>
<ul>
    <li>Click on a token you want to move</li>
    <li>A token will be highlighted with a pink square in a middle. Click in the pink square</li>
    <li>Click on the big token to rotate it 90 degrees.</li>
    <li>One you have your chosen rotation click outside the big token</li>
</ul>
